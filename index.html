What it does

Local files, organized

Browse by folder, album, or artist. Import a folder straight from Google Drive if that's where your files live.

Books read aloud

Import a PDF or EPUB and Audex reads it aloud with a real offline voice — no internet needed once the voice is downloaded.

Android Auto

Browse and play everything from the car, with a proper queue, shuffle, and resume.
